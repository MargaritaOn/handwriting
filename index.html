How to improve your handwriting

Tip #2

A poor pencil grip can affect the quality of handwriting as well as putting unnecessary strain on the hand muscles and ligaments. This causes the hand to tense or cramp and tire quickly, making the handwriting process hard work.

Tip #1

Poor posture can be a root cause of common handwriting difficulties such as pain or fatigue and will present as a reluctance to write, poor presentation or legibility, slow writing speed and reduced output.

Interesting facts:

  • The earliest known handwriting was on clay tablets in Mesopotamia around 3,300BC.
  • The ancient Greeks and Romans were the first to use joined-up (cursive) writing.
  • The US Declaration of Independence in 1776 was originally handwritten.
  • About 3,000 UK businesses use handwriting analysis as part of their selection procedures. In the USA, such graphology, as it is called, is illegal.
